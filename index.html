Mary Oyibo Ogbonna

1930-2020


Mary Oyibo Ogbonna lived a very peaceful and selfless life. A lovely wife, wonderful and caring mother, awesome grandma and a Great-Grandma

Born in 1930 was an Icon of her time.
Died in 2020 at the age of 90 Yrs is a true symbol of a selfless being. She is greatly missed.
